Our Story

Born in a Kitchen.
Built in a Lab.

Chotulabs started with a simple obsession: why does every hot sauce taste the same? Our founder Arjun spent two years — and nearly his sense of taste — experimenting in his small (chotu!) apartment kitchen in Kochi.

In 2022, with nothing but a pressure cooker, a pH meter, and a reckless love for chillies, Chotulabs was born. Today we ship to over 200 cities across India, but every batch is still handcrafted, still numbered, still small.

Because when food is made with obsession, you can taste the difference.
